Campus Ambassador

About

Raag, NIT Goa is the first edition of cultural festival. Embodying the true spirit of youth, Raag provides a platform for young talent from all over India to showcase their varied talents.

Now, you too have a chance to become a part of this cultural extravaganza. With Campus Ambassador Program you get a chance to be an extended part of the organising team of Raag 2017 along with many incentives!

Duties of a Campus Ambassador

Raag 2017 promtoes the Campus Ambassador Program to incorporate participation from students spread across the nation in various colleges.
A Campus Ambassador is an official representative of Raag 2017 in his/her college. As a Campus Ambassador you shall be coordinating with Raag to ensure maximum participation at Raag from your college.
This can be achieved through:

  • Word of Mouth
  • Posters
  • Online publicity of Raag through social media in your city and especially in your college
  • Helping out Raag to reach out to your college in any manner you deem fit.

This is a good opportunity for students looking to undertake a position of responsibility and gain an exposure to the much needed communication and managerial skills. Campus Ambassadors are an important part of the extended family of Raag which is acknowledged with a certificate from the National Institute of Technology Goa, along with other perks!

Perks of a Campus Ambassador

  • Certificate of Organisation
  • Recognization on webpage
  • Bring in maximum participation and prizes worth 3000/- up for grabs!
