All Goa Science Fair

Date: 20th August 2016
Venue: NIT Goa Campus

In association with SPIE (The international society for optics and photonics) and LMTx (Laser Mission 10x), Raag celebrates the fact that science and technology meets culture through this event. Exclusively for students of grade 8-10, the event will comprise of preparing a working or still model of certain science and technology related categories. We are trying to reach out to the middle school students of Goa, to identify their inner potential in innovative thinking and interest in science and technology. It is also to inspire and nurture them in this field, as they are the building blocks of a progressive society. This event will be followed by a series of cultural performances to enthrall the audience.

Make a Change

Date: August – December

Raag strives to target the youth with social issues. With social initiatives like the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan, Rashtriya Avishkar Abhiyan and Unnat Bharat Abhiyan, Raag makes it a point to ’Make a Change’ in the society. During the span of August to December, we will be hosting 4 Unnat Bharat Abhiyan and Rashtriya Avishkar Abhiyan sessions in 4 different schools in and around Goa and 2 Swachh Bharat Abhiyan sessions at the most prime locations of Goa.
