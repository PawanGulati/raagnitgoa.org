RulesPicasso

  • Individual Entry
  • The contestant shall caption the artwork appropriately
  • The contestant shall only use lead pencils and white shade pencils(if required) only
  • Each contestant shall submit one entry only
  • The dimensions of the entry shall not exceed standard A3 size sheets
  • The artwork shall be a part of the art/photo gallery exhibition
  • The entries are to be received by the organizers on or before 26th January 2017
  • Mandatory details which are to be provided:
    • 1. Name
    • 2. Phone No
    • 3. College Name
    • 4. Theme
  • The hardcopy of the original artwork must be submitted directly to the organizers or post it to the given address:
    • Picasso Organizers
    • Raag 2017
    • National Institute of Technology Goa
    • Farmagudi, Ponda, North Goa, Goa, India
    • Pin: 403401
  • The decision of the judges is final and binding
