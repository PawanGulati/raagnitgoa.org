RulesBattle of The Bands

  • Team Composition: 4-8
  • There shall be only one round
  • Original compositions or renditions, use of Indian instruments will fetch extra marks
  • At the end of 12.5 minutes of the performance, yellow indication will be shown, reminding the contestants to conclude the performance in 2 minutes
  • At the end of 14.5 minutes of the performance, red indication will be shown, reminding the contestants to conclude the performance in the following grace time of 30 seconds
  • The total time of the performance shall not exceed 15 minutes (including set up and clearance time). Failure to abide will result in disqualification/reduction of marks
  • The songs shall be in English/Regional Indian Languages
  • In case of original compositions, participants need to provide a copy of the lyrics to the organisers before the event
  • Instruments to be provided - a Drum Set, an Electric Guitar, a Keyboard and a Guitar Amplifier
  • Any harm to the instrument while performance would be compensated by the team
  • The lyrics shall not contain any vulgarity or abusive words
  • There shall be no pre-recorded music
  • The judge's decision is final and binding
