RulesChunin Exam

  • Team Composition: 3 mandatory
  • Round 1 shall be a written test
  • Team(s) who meet the criteria mentioned in the questionnaire shall proceed to round 2 which shall comprise of tasks to be performed as a team
  • Team(s) who meet the criteria mentioned in round 2 shall proceed to round 3 which shall comprise of tasks to be performed against other team(s)
  • The test shall be prepared by the organizers
  • The organizers decision is final and binding
