RulesRaag Quiz

  • Team Composition: 3
  • The quiz will be conducted based on the following categories
    • 1.   Music
    • 2.   Entertainment
    • 3.   Literature
    • 4.   Arts
    • 5.   Sports
  • The preliminaries of the quiz will be based on written questionnaire. The top five teams shall qualify to the final round
  • The final round will be conducted on stage. Questions in the final round will include verbal, audio clips, video rounds etc and would cover other subjects and even beyond
  • The format of the rounds would be left to the discretion of the Quiz Masters
  • The quizmaster's decision is final and binding
