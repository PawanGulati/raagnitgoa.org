RulesFutsal

  • Team Composition: 5+2
  • A team shall contain minimum of 5 players (4 outfield players and a goalkeeper) or a maximum of 7 players
  • The game consist of a half of 6 minutes
  • The objective of the game is to get the ball in the back of the opposition goal as many times as possible
  • A goal may be scored from any point within the field of play including when taking corners
  • Goal cannot be scored directly from the kick-off
  • If the game ends as a draw, best of 3 penalty kicks will be taken and if no winner then sudden death (one player to score and one player to miss)
  • Slide tackle will be not allowed
  • There are no off-side rules
  • There will be a maximum of 5 players per team on the field at any one time (4 outfield players and a goalkeeper)
  • Rolling substitutions
  • The players can pass the ball back to the goalkeeper with no restrictions on number of touches
  • A change of goalkeeper must be authorized by the referee
  • No abusing the referee - we're all here to have fun!
  • The player may receive yellow card or a straight red card for serious offense
  • Two yellow cards for same player will result into a red card
  • A red carded player will have to leave the field of play for the entire game
