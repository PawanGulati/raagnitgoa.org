RulesZest

  • Team Composition: 4-8
  • Each team should submit their theme of performance and introductory speech (If required) prior to the commencement of the event
  • The participants shall not speak or sing
  • Pre-recorded music is permitted
  • The performance should not include any vulgarity
  • The use of prop(s) is permitted
  • At the end of 4.5 minutes of the performance, yellow indication will be used, reminding the contestants to conclude the performance in 60 seconds
  • The total time limit of the performance shall be 6 minutes. Failure to abide will result in disqualification
  • The judge's grading is final and binding
