RulesStep up

  • Team Composition: 3-8 (Minimum of 3 and Maximum of 8)
  • The grading shall be based on the following categories respectively:
    • Western Dance
    • Cinematic Dance
    • Contemporary Dance
    • Classical Dance
  • Each team should mention their respective choice of category
  • No participant is allowed to compete for two different teams
  • The overall highest scoring team(s) will be declared the winner
  • Western dancers shall wear western attire and shoes. Songs shall be in English
  • Cinematic dancers shall choose any film song (original/renditions) from Indian languages
  • Classical dancers shall submit their sub-category to the organizers prior to the event
  • Contemporary dancers shall choose any combinations of the same
  • The use of props are permitted
  • At the end of 4.5 minutes of the performance, yellow indication will be used, reminding the contestants to conclude the performance in 60 seconds
  • At the end of 5.5 minutes of the performance, red indication will be used, reminding the contestants to conclude the performance in the following grace time of 30 seconds.
  • The total time of the performance shall not exceed 6 minutes. Failure to abide will result in disqualification
  • The judge's decision is final and binding
