RulesFootloose

  • Individual Entry
  • The grading shall be based on the following categories respectively:
    • Western Dance
    • Cinematic Dance
    • Contemporary Dance
    • Classical Dance
  • Each contestant should mention their respective choice of category prior to the commencement of the event
  • The soundtracks shall be submitted to the event organizer in a pen drive, prior to the event
  • Use of props is permitted
  • At the end of 4.5 minutes of the performance, yellow indication will be used, reminding the contestants to conclude the performance in 60 seconds
  • At the end of 5.5 minutes of the performance, red indication will be used, reminding the contestants to conclude the performance in the following grace time of 30 seconds
  • The total time of the performance shall not exceed 6 minutes. Failure to abide will result in disqualification/reduction of marks
  • The judge's decision is final and binding
