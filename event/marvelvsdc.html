RulesMarvel VS DC

  • Team Composition: 2 maximum
  • Each team shall comprise of 2 participants. One among the two shall be the painter
  • Half of the face of the other contestant shall be painted based on any character from Marvel and the other half based on any character from DC
  • The completion time for the same shall be 3 hours
  • Contestants have to commence the painting on site, else entries will not be permitted
  • The contestants shall use any face painting means
  • The use of masks or props are not permitted
  • The grading of the judges are final and binding
