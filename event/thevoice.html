RulesThe Voice

REGIONAL

  • Individual Entry
  • Only regional songs are permitted (Indian languages)
  • In case the participants wish to use backing track, they shall submit it via pen-drive
  • ONLY ONE accompanying instrument is allowed. The instrument must be played by the singer themselves
  • At the end of 3.5 minutes of the performance, yellow indication will be used, reminding the contestants to conclude the performance in 60 seconds
  • At the end of 4.5 minutes of the performance, red indication will be used, reminding the contestants to conclude the performance in the following grace time of 30 seconds.
  • The total time of the performance shall not exceed 5 minutes. Failure to abide will result in disqualification
  • The judge's grading is final and binding
